Training of Trainers:
Understanding Climate Risk for
Evidence-Based Planning

1 Introduction

Times and Dates

January 11, 2023 5.30-8 pm IST

January 12, 2023 5.30-8 pm IST

Location

Virtual Training at KILA Headquarters in Thrissur

Tools Needed

Please download QGIS onto your computer before the training. QGIS will be needed to complete the technical exercise and to earn the certificate of completion.

Training Objective

  • Participants will develop a baseline understanding of climate risk analysis and the components, applications, and limitations of climate modeling and mapping.

  • Participants will be able to use geospatial data to help tell their stories.
